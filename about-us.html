ABOUT US

We adhere to a clear set of values that will drive our operating principles, business ambitions and are fundamental to the way we are working.

Willber Construction Ltd. Well Services , (Willber) a work-over and well service company, was formed in 2006 by a group with extensive experience in the techniques of work-over and well services - expertise that sets Willber Construction Ltd. apart from any other company in the Middle East.

Our highly motivated, experienced crews operate the most innovative Snubbing & Work Over Rigs, Pre-Construction units, Nitrogen & Fluid Pumping Equipment and remedial and thru-tubing tools available in the industry today. Today Willber Construction Ltd. is operating in United Arab Emirates, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Oman and US.

Willber’s profile

Willber Well Services LLC, (Willber) a Snubbing & Work-Over, Pre-Construction and Well Intervention company, was formed in 2006 in the Oman by a group of industry expects with extensive experience in the techniques of snubbing & work-over, Coiled tubing and well Intervention Services - expertise that sets Willber Construction Company Ltd. apart from any other company in the Middle East region Our highly motivated, experienced crews operate the most innovative snubbing & work over systems, coiled tubing units, fishing tools and thru-tubing tools available in the industry today - predominately built and developed by Willber Construction Ltd..

Since its inception, Willber Construction Ltd. has strategically grown from a small well service company to a company with a fleet of sophisticated thru-tubing tools, Pre-Construction units and advanced snubbing & work over units. To better supply the customers with oilfield servicing solutions, the company added specialized fishing tools, thru-tubing cutting systems, and scale removal systems to its portfolio. With its unique expertise, Willber Construction Ltd. also provided support to drilling operations by supplying equipment and personnel on many well service operations within the oil and gas industry.

Willber Construction Ltd. has grown both in its home market and outside of the Middle East region and leads the way in thru-tubing interventions, coiled tubing services and snubbing & work over innovations.

The philosophy is to develop the best solutions in close cooperation with our clients, and to establish strong, long term relations by implementing quality in all the company did. Quality to Willber Construction Ltd. is when we meet or supersede our clients expectations, both in technical skills and in customer follow up.

The company’s core products are based on the experience and skills of our employees, and we firmly believe that taking well care of our internal human resources will be reflected in professional merits and customer satisfaction. Our focus is on offering optimal solution to our customers and to achieve long term customer satisfaction.

Willber’s experience

Willber Construction Ltd. is one of the most experienced work over and well operation contractors in the region today. Our management and supervisory team offer more than 20 years combined experience in the field. In fact, even today all of Willber’s management team still participates in the day-to-day field operations when needed - a level of service that is greatly valued by our customers. Willber Construction Ltd. employed a team of highly skilled equipment operators - considered the best in the industry - that have built Willber’s reputation for quality service and safety. As an industry leader, Willber Construction Ltd. has completed numerous jobs for major oil companies in the region.
