Welcome to Willber Construction Ltd

Willber Construction was formed in 2006 by a group with extensive experience in the techniques of work-over and well services - expertise that sets Willber Construction Ltd. apart from any other company in the Middle East.

Our highly motivated, experienced crews operate the most innovative Snubbing & Work Over Rigs, Pre-Construction units, Nitrogen & Fluid Pumping Equipment and remedial and thru-tubing tools available in the industry today. Today Willber Construction Ltd. is operating in United Arab Emirates, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Oman and US.
